Services Offered

Prices vary by size, coat condition, and behavior. Any price quotes given before the groom are approximate and subject to change.

Bath, Brush, Nails

Service includes a bath with gentle shampoo. After drying your poochie gets a thorough brushing and combing to remove excess fur and minor mat removal if needed. Nails are trimmed and ears are cleaned. Any long hair on the feet, eye area, sanitaries, or ear openings is trimmed. A light all over trim can be added to this service for breeds like Golden Retrievers, Shelties, Australian Shepherds, and Pomeranians. We finish up with a scented cologne and bandana or bow. Anal glands can be externally expressed by request only.

Full Grooming

(hair cut)

Service includes everything in a Bath, Brush, Nail service with the addition of a nose to tail hair cut customized for your poochies needs and your style preference. While we are skilled in mat removal we will take the dogs comfort into account. We prefer to put humanity over vanity. If you desire a long, fluffy look we recommend frequent visits, coat type can vary this as some dogs need every other week brushing visits while others can go 6 weeks. Mat removal is an additional charge based on time.

What counts as a special needs or senior pet?

When we talk about these we are referring to dogs that may have sound sensitivity, say other dogs barking make them nervous, or some senior pets have difficulty standing and require two people to make the groom more comfortable. For these dogs we set aside a more private time with less interruptions and noise. Tuesdays are THE BEST days for them, with Saturday being THE WORST. We have experience in working with timid rescue dogs, dogs with disabilities like paralysis or seizures, and seniors who may have the onset of doggy dementia. These precious poochies will be worked straight through and made comfortable throughout their visit. .
