Pet Groomers

Katrina started her grooming career in 2007 in a well established facility in Panama City, Florida. She worked there for around 7 years learning under a seasoned groomer who herself had learned to groom from attending dog shows with her hounds and learned from the breeders and handlers of their specific breeds. This allowed her to learn the official show grooms as well as quality pet grooms that gave to look of the show ring without the daily maintenance. Katrina gained many, many, years of grooming knowledge and techniques while apprenticing under an amazing teacher. Katrina, and her husband Thomas, opened Pristine Poochies in June of 2018 to provide excellent, personalized grooming services to the Savannah area. (photo of Thomas, Katrina, Sitka, and Frida)
